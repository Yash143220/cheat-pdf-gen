How to Print on Both Sides (Front & Back)

1

Open Print Dialog

Open your downloaded PDF and press Ctrl+P (Windows) or Cmd+P (Mac)

2

Enable Two-Sided Printing

Look for options like:

  • "Two-Sided" or "Front & Back"
  • "Print on Both Sides"
  • "Layout: Two-Sided"
3

Select "Flip on Long Edge"

CRITICAL: Choose "Long Edge" NOT "Short Edge"
